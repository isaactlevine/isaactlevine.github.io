Harvest

Harvest is an application that uses AI (machine vision) to enable users to easily capture and organize information from across devices and platforms. Harvest lets you take screenshots or photos of anything - recipes, books, signs, events, locations - and automatically extracts the key details to create clean records that are then logged to a vector store for semantic retrieval.

As technical lead and product strategist, I oversaw development of Harvest's core image processing and machine learning capabilities, as well as app development. I crafted an intuitive user flow that made it effortless to build a personal knowledge base from daily life. My strategies shaped core product directions - focusing first on privacy-preserving on-device processing, and emphasizing speed and utility over complex social features.

The app is currently not public and in beta-testing, so please contact me directly for more information.
